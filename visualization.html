CSO EAA17 · High Value Dataset

How have Ireland's Greenhouse Gas Emissions evolved by economic sector?

Interactive dashboard built from CSO Environmental Accounts EAA17 (2009–2023). Explore sectoral trends, gas composition, and structural change across all NACE sectors.

Analytical focus

What questions does this answer?

  • Which sectors currently emit the most GHGs?
  • How has gas composition (CO₂, CH₄, N₂O…) shifted over time?
  • Which sectors have reduced or increased emissions the most?
  • Which gases are driving change in specific sectors?

Tip: click any bar to cross-highlight that sector across all charts. Double-click to reset.

How to use the controls

Interactive controls

  • Selected Year — filters the Top Sectors bar chart
  • Gas Type — filters the Structural Change and Scatter charts
  • Baseline / Comparison Year — sets the two years for % change calculation

The structural change chart ranks the 15 sectors with the largest absolute % shift between your two chosen years.

Dataset & processing

Data source

Source: CSO Environmental Accounts EAA17 — GHG emissions by NACE sector and gas type (kt CO₂e), 2009–2023.

Pre-processing: Filtered to 2009–2023, standardised column names, one record per (Year × Sector × Gas) combination. Saved as eaa17_processed.csv on GitHub for full reproducibility. All aggregation is done inside Vega-Lite; raw values are not modified.
